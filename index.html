Mini-lennium Falcon

Start

Plow your way through space in hyperspeed with the Mini-lennium Falcon (or YT-1200)!

But be careful to avoid the incoming asteroids & other potential enemy cruisers that you may run into.

Collect the blaster cannon powerups along the way to collect ammunition.

How To Play

*Hold 'Spacebar' to stay afloat.
*Press 'Enter' to shoot.
*Press 'S' to toggle sound.
