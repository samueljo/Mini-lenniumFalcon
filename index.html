Mini-lennium Falcon

Start

Plow your way through space with Chewbacca & Han in hyperspeed with the Mini-lennium Falcon (or YT-1200)!

But be careful to avoid the incoming asteroids.

Collect the blaster cannon powerups along the way to collect ammunition.

How To Play

* Hold 'Spacebar' to stay afloat *
* Press 'Enter' to shoot *
* Press 'S' to toggle sound *
